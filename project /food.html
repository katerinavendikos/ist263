Food Recommendations from a Local

Brooklyn is a large borough. There are so many places to enjoy food and conversation. However, there are a few places that are home to native Brooklynites.
The first place I will highlight is L&B Spumoni Gardens, home of the delicious Sicilian square pizza and spumoni ice cream. L&B Spumoni Gardens has been serving pizza and spumoni since 1939. Originating on 86th street in Brooklyn, there are now three locations that are thriving. L&B Spumoni Gardens is a proudly family-owned business, owned and loved by the Barbati family. The third location also has a dining room for more refined Italian plates.
