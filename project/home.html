Welcome to Brooklyn

How Sweet It Is!

Home to 2.59 million people, Brooklyn is the sweetest place to be. Located outside of Manhattan, over the Brooklyn Bridge--or the Williamsburg Bridge, or the Hugh L. Carrey Tunnel, or even the Manhattan Bridge is a world full of different cultures, histories, and experiences

This website will be able to give you a small slice of things to do here in our little slice of New York! Perhaps you'll want to have some pizza or go to a museum. You might just want to take a walk around. We can help with that!

Enjoy your stay! Once you enter, when you think of leaving, you'll say "fuhgeddaboudit!"
