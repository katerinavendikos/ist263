Fun Things To Do

Prospect Park

When it is warm out, grab a friend and walk around the park! Spanning 586 acres, you will not even feel like you are in Brooklyn anymore. There is even a zoo and iceskating in the winter.

Brooklyn Museum

Take a walk around the Brooklyn Museum! Here, you can view beautiful works of art. There is ancient art on display, as well as modern art. Here is the link to the website so you can see what exhibits are available.
